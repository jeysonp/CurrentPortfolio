About Me

When I was in High School I discovered that I feel the best when I am being creative in some way. I took a photography class, painting, ceramics, and choir. I went to college at Kansas State University and earned a degree in Pre-Art Therapy. I loved college. I loved creating art. I also found that I love working with people. I never made it to grad school. However, in that year I found a love for computers. I eventually found a new career in IT, specifically desktop and network support. I enjoyed my job, but it felt a little stagnate at the time, so I started looking for a change.

Life went on and I needed more income to support my family. An opportunity came along for a sales job with a large national moving company. I have been very successful in sales. I was even one of the top three residential sales people in the country for Mayflower in 2015. However, I have come to realize that I miss the creative side of myself. I think a part of me is missing. I also miss working in the tech field.

Now, I am hoping to combine two of my favorite interests and start a new career in web development. I am so excited and nervous. When I went to school the first time, I did not have a demanding job, a wife, and 3 daughters to share my time with. However, my family has been very supportive. I hope to find a career soon, and an outlet to be creative as well.
